Avians

Broad-tailed hummingbird

Known to build its nests at altitudes over 10,000 feet!

  • Scientific Name: Selasphorus platycercus
  • Average Length: 4"
  • Average Lifespan: 10 years
  • Habitat: Mountains

A long-bodied hummingbird, with bright pink legs and a black bill. Has a goose-like honk for a call.

Greater flamingo

Feeds by sucking up water through its bill and filtering out organisms.

  • Scientific Name: Phoenicopterus roseus
  • Average Length: 43-59"
  • Average Lifespan: 60 years
  • Habitat: Lagoons

A tall, pinkish-white bird, with males displaying a deep red throat and iridescent green back.

Fish

Yellow tang

At night, their yellow color fades and a noticeable brown patch develops in the middle of their body.

  • Scientific Name: Zebrasoma flavescens
  • Average Length: 7"
  • Average Lifespan: 30 years (wild), 5-10 years (captivity)
  • Habitat: Saltwater

A brilliantly yellow fish very common in aquariums.

Mammals

Red fox

Foxes live on every continent except Antarctica.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average Length: 18-35"
  • Average Lifespan: 5 years (wild), 15 years (captivity)
  • Habitat: Any

The largest of all the foxes, whose widespread population is often deemed an invasive species.
